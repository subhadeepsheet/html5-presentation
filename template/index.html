The Catch

Simply: Legacy browsers do not support these features.

  • Also: This is completely fine.

Graceful degradation

Our sites will look awesome in the latest/most standard-compliants browsers, and will still look good in older browsers.

Graceful degradation

  • Retain functionality, alter execution

Graceful degradation

Still legible and functional

  • Not too bad right?

Do:

  • Use advanced techniques
  • Degrade gracefully
  • Be okay with degradation
  • This means you, designer!

Don’t:

  • Worry (be happy)
  • Provide overly-complicated fallbacks
  • Over-emphasize this point. Make it status quo. We’ll buy you beers.*
* or whiskey or wine or tea

The web evolves. We should too.
